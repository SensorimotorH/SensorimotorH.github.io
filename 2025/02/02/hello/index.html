About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Ocean University of China(Majors ranked 1/66) and will graduate in June 2025. In September 2024, I was accepted by Beihang University as a master's candidate.

My undergraduate research was mainly focused on mechanical design in the field of robotics. In the future, I will focus on the field of machine learning (specifically, the application of machine learning in natural language processing and image processing), hoping to achieve good results.
